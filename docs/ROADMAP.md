# TrailKit Roadmap

## Phase 0: Manual/Fake MVP

- Smart links with source type, campaign, partner, and destination metadata.
- Click tracking through `/r/[slug]`.
- Manual revenue matching from imported or mocked events.
- Founder interviews with app teams running creators, affiliates, emails, website buttons, and QR campaigns.
- No full SDK yet.

## Phase 1: Sellable MVP

- Smart link builder.
- App store routing.
- Click tracking.
- Basic first-open attribution model.
- RevenueCat webhook-ready integration design.
- Source-level dashboard.
- Manual payout calculations.
- CSV export for partner payout review.

## Phase 2: Strong MVP

- iOS and Android support plan.
- RevenueCat, Adapty, Apphud, and Superwall support plan.
- Partner dashboards.
- QR codes.
- Custom domains.
- Webhooks.
- Payout exports.
- Event replay and audit log.

## Phase 3: Growth Infrastructure

- Deferred deep linking.
- Fraud checks.
- Stripe Connect payouts.
- API access.
- Campaign cohorts.
- SDK-assisted first-open matching.
- Cohort revenue by link, source, and partner.

## Phase 4: Bigger Platform

- Agency tools.
- Multi-app workspaces.
- Advanced attribution analytics.
- Partner marketplace only if validated.
- Advanced billing, permissions, and reporting.
- SKAdNetwork and privacy-preserving attribution research if demanded by customers.
